The Crater Floor Campaign, conducted from May 2021 (Sol 100) to April 2022 (Sol 400), focused on the systematic exploration of the *Maaz* and *Seitah* formations. 

### Geological Context
- *Seitah* Formation: Represents the lowest elevation sampled by Perseverance. It is characterized by mafic rock with high abundances of olivine and pyroxene.
- *Maaz* Formation: Overlies the Séítah formation, comprised of a more varied felsic-mafic mixture with lower abundances of olivine and pyroxene.

### Campaign Info
- Duration: ~300 Sols.
- Distance Traversed: ~5 km prior to Delta Front arrival.
- Primary Minerals: Olivine, Pyroxene, Feldspar.
- Key Targets: Dourbes, Quartier, Bellegarde.
