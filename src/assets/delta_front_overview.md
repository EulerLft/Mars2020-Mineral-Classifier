The Delta Front Campaign began in April 2022 (Sol 400), marking a transition from the igneous crater floor to the ancient sedimentary fan deposits of Jezero Crater. This phase of the mission is dedicated to exploring layered rocks deposited by a river system billions of years ago.

### Campaign Info
- Duration: ~700+ Sols (ongoing since Sol 400).
- Distance Traversed: ~15+ km total distance reached within the delta.
- Primary Minerals: Alteration/High Alteation.
- Key Targets: Dourbes, Quartier, Bellegarde.
